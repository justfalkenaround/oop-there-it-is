O.O.P THERE IT IS

Welcome to a tutorial intended to strengthen your understanding of some exciting and powerful programming concepts.

USEFUL O.O.P, ANIMATION, AND GAME DESIGN

This course is intended to be completed sequentially, starting at lesson one, to the end. Each lesson builds upon the last to gradually reinforce and introduce new concepts. There are also additional supplementary lessons for various categories. This course can be administered in a classroom setting where students are lectured and then split into small groups to complete the challenge at the end of each lesson. Or a student may complete it on their own. Either way, don't forget to use your resources. That means having dev-docs open all the time, and working with others to solve problems.

WARNING: A basic understanding of JAVASCRIPT is recommended before undertaking this course. This course is intended to be friendly to all levels of coder but having a base knowledge of JAVASCRIPT will likely make it much more valuable to you.
